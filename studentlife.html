Who are we?

By the students, for the students

The Edinburgh University Student Association (EUSA) is your student union at the University. The EUSA is a student led, campaigned, and funded organisation created for your benefit. The organisation has a multitude of resources available for you to be invovled, innovative, and empowered to do whatever you can to make your university experience that much better.

Get involved!

The EUSA is your gateway to finding out your personal community and your chance to build your impact on the University as a whole! You will discover a huge network of organisations called societies here at Edinburgh that will grow your interests and profession into something more. New students are welcome because without you, the EUSA is nothing!

To learn more about the EUSA, click here!

Societies

Your passions realised

Societies are student ran special interest organisations that bring your hobbies and passions to the greater university community. These are a great way to find like minded people and further cultivate your passions and interests.

For everyone and anyone

There is always a soceity for anyone. Whether you are an afficiando of photography or an amateur programmer wanting to get into game design, societies are inclusive and low barrier groups that let you collaborate and explore your interests or find your next passion. The university makes sure that these societies are low cost so that you can focus more on the activities rather than the funding!

Explore and be open

Searching and joining a society is a streamlined process! All societies have a wonderful crew of officials that are easy to contact and everyone manages their events through social media. Use this to find your societies to join!

Events

Work hard, play hard

We understand that every student needs an appropriate academic and social life balance to be mentally healthy and to get the most out of the university experience. The EUSA constantly and consistently provides students activites to do throughout the year as a community. Whether it be watching a sports game at the Library Bar or enjoying a comedy show, the University always has something available to do! Check out what's going on today here!

Fresher's Week

The biggest week for new students yet! This is the university's way of introducing you to the vibrant community at the University of Edinburgh as well as the academic life for every one to enjoy! There is no better way see Edinburgh like this, so check out the calendar here. From September 10th to the 17th, enjoy a multitude of activities such as a castle tour of Edinburgh or a visit to the zoo or go on a pub crawl! All these activities require money at the door, but if you buy a Fresher's wristband you'll enjoy discounted or even free events. This the best way to enjoy everything you can this fresher's week!
